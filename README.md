Amazon Product Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

# Project Overview

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze Amazon product data, focusing on product performance, customer ratings, pricing trends, and discount analysis. The dashboard enables users to identify top-performing products, evaluate category-wise performance, and uncover pricing insights through dynamic visualizations.

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Excel / CSV Dataset

## Dashboard Pages

 1. Overview

* Analyzed 1,000+ products across multiple categories.
* Tracked key KPIs including Average Rating, Average Selling Price, Average Discount %, and Total Products.
* Visualized category-wise product distribution and ratings.

 2. Product Analysis

* Identified top-rated products based on customer ratings.
* Analyzed the most reviewed products using rating counts.
* Highlighted products offering the highest discounts.

 3. Price Analysis

* Compared discount percentages across product categories.
* Evaluated the relationship between ratings and discounts.
* Calculated total customer savings from discounted prices.

## Key Insights

* Electronics and Computer Accessories contain the highest number of products.
* Several products receive high customer engagement through ratings and reviews.
* Discount percentages vary significantly across categories.
* Customers collectively save millions through discounted pricing offers.
